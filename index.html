Luigi Muci

PhD Student · African Cultural Studies · University of Wisconsin-Madison

Hello! I'm Luigi. My research explores intersections between culture and nation. Specifically, I observe the ways in which expressive cultural forms reflect and contrast ideas of the 'nation' within a postcolonial context. My research has focused on the cultural history of Sierra Leone particularly dance, theatre and literature as iterations and expressions of national cultural identity.

I am second year PhD student in the Department of African Cultural Studies, University of Wisconsin-Madison, USA. I hold an MSc in African Studies from the University of Oxford and a BA in History from University College London-UC Berkeley.

I am always earger to build connection with fellow scholars and explore new areas of knowledge.
