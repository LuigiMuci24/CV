Luigi Muci

PhD Student · African Cultural Studies · University of Wisconsin-Madison

Hello! I'm Luigi. I research the ways nations are imagined within and through expressive culture.

I am currently a PhD student at the Department of African Cultural Studies, University of Wisconsin-Madison, USA. I hold an MSc in African Studies from the University of Oxford, with a passion for interdisciplinary approaches to literature, music, and the arts in relation to national identity.

My work explores how storytelling, performance, and media shape ideas of belonging and community, especially across African contexts. I'm always eager to collaborate, share ideas, or discuss new projects!
